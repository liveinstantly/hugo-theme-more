Hugo More is a theme for the Hugo static site generator that's specifically designed for simple and unversal websites, which can be applied to multiple purposes, such as company introduction, documentation, blog, and so on.

Our aim is to help you get a working website up and running as easily as possible, so you can concentrate on creating great content for your users.
